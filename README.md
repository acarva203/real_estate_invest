Strategic Housing Development
The UC Davis Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) is a STEM-designated program based in San Francisco that equips professionals with the expertise to turn complex data into actionable business insights. The program combines advanced technical tools with live practicum projects for corporate sponsors, enabling students to leverage data science to solve real-world challenges and drive organizational change.

Your Mission
A real estate firm seeks to identify the best locations for new single-family homes targeting households aged 25 to 45 with incomes over $100,000. Your challenge is to analyze US Census data to determine which zip codes offer the highest potential for growth through 2030. Evaluate age and income demographics alongside time-series trends to create an executive dashboard or presentation. Your solution must provide data-driven recommendations on where the company should invest in future housing developments.
